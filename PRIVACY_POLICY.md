# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how CleanShare handles information when you use the app.

## Information We Collect

CleanShare does not collect, store, or transmit personal information to any server.

The app processes files locally on your device in order to rename them and remove metadata before sharing.

## How We Use Information

Any file content selected by you is used only to provide the app’s core functionality:

- Renaming files
- Removing metadata
- Preparing files for sharing

## Data Storage and Sharing

- Files are processed on-device.
- The app does not upload files to external servers.
- The app does not sell, rent, or share personal information.

## Third-Party Services

CleanShare does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children’s Privacy

CleanShare is not intended to collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this repository.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please refer to the project repository or contact the maintainer through GitHub.
